As you can see I like collecting plants with different colors/patterns.

I have a particular preference for silvery plants.

It's too expensive to a lot of the plants I collect as full plants, so I usually buy cuttings/nodes from other collectors. My favorites are philodendrons and scindapsus varieties.

All of my Pets:

  • I keep a pet red eared slider named Mr. Nobody, his full name is Sheldon Nobody (Trachemys scripta elegans)

  • I also keep colony of Dwarf Red Cherry Shrimp in a aquascape, they are collectively called Spork courtesy of Grace (Neocaridina Davidi)

    • These shrimp breed like rabbits, so I sometimes sell some online
  • Lastly I have Sesame, my little zebra nerite who keeps the glass clean (Neritina natalensis)
